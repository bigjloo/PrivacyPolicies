Privacy Policy

We collect personal information from you, including information about your:

  • Name
  • Contact information
  • Location
  • IP address
  • Date and time of visit to the site
  • Pages accessed and documents downloaded
  • Operating system
  • Type of browser

We collect your personal information in order to:

  • provide support for administrative tasks [1]

We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted files and have proper user access management in place [2].
We keep your information for 90 days at which point we securely destroy it by securely erasing all digital copies. [4]

Only aggregated information is shared for statistical purposes [5]

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong [3].
If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at:

 
  • customerservices@openpolytechnic.ac.nz
  • 0508 650 200
  • Private Bag 31914 Lower Hutt 5040 New Zealand.

[1] of the privacy policy clearly states the purpose of collecting the personal information which adhere to privacy principle 3. For principle 5, which concerns the storage and security of personal information, we can see from [2] that the agency uses encrypted files and has set up user access management in place to prevent unauthorized misuse. [3] of the privacy policy informs the user of their rights to have their personal information corrected if they think it is wrong (privacy principle 7). To prevent holding information longer than necessary (privacy principle 9), the privacy police states that the agency only keeps the personal information for 90 days which it is then securely erased [4]. Section [5] adheres to principle 11, which does not identify any individual.
